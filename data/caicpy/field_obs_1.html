BC Zone Observation Report

Friday, January 20, 2023 at 12:00 AM
Vail & Summit County

Details

  • Date: 2023/01/20
  • Observer: Drew Gibson
  • Organization: Forecaster CAIC

Location

  • BC Zone: Vail & Summit County
  • Area Description: Boss Basin, Vail Pass

Weather

  • Ridgeline Wind Speed: 5-10 mph
  • Ridgeline Wind Direction: NE
  • Wind Loading: Light
  • Sky Cover: Scattered
  • Weather Description: Cold and mostly calm with high clouds increasing through the day. Higher peaks had signs of more easterly winds in the morning while on approach to the trailhead.

Snowpack

  • Snowpack Description: There was evidence of wind-loading on upper elevation slopes lee to the WNW. On the above and near treeline slopes we traveled on, the fresh load was soft (Fist to 4 Finger hard) and no cracking was observed. Near and below treeline the snowpack was supportive even on hard turns despite a approximate 1m depth. On wind sheltered slopes there was 20cm-35cm of new snow that was bonding well to the old snow. Pit data showed a snowpack comprised of mostly soft but supportable facets with moderate compression test failures on the basal facets. A Propagation Saw Test on the same layer had a 40/100 END failure. The only avalanche activity noted were a few small pockets (less than 10m x 10m) on steep rollovers with notable shallow areas.
